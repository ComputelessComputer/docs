Anarlog is an open-source AI meeting notetaker that captures system audio from any meeting platform, transcribes on-device or with your preferred provider, and generates AI-powered summaries using the LLM of your choice. Every note is saved as a plain Markdown file on your disk — you own your data completely.

## Why Anarlog?

Anarlog takes a different approach from most meeting tools: everything runs on your machine. There is no bot joining your call, no audio uploaded to a cloud, and no account to sign up for. Your meeting recordings, transcripts, and notes stay in a folder you choose.

## Get Started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Download Anarlog">
    Grab the latest release for macOS from [GitHub Releases](https://github.com/fastrepl/anarlog/releases/latest). Apple Silicon and Intel builds are both available.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Grant permissions">
    On first launch, Anarlog will ask for microphone and screen-recording permissions so it can capture system audio from your meetings.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure your AI provider">
    Open **Settings → Intelligence** and add an API key for OpenAI, Anthropic, or any provider you prefer. You can also run a local model with Ollama or LM Studio — no key needed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start a meeting note">
    Create a new note, join your meeting, and click **Start listening**. Anarlog records and transcribes in the background while you take notes. When the call ends, click **Enhance** to generate your AI summary.
  </Step>
</Steps>
